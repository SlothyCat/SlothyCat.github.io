# Motivation
I wanted to build an **expense tracker** that can help me to export to a `Google Sheet` so 
that I would be able to manipulate and visualise the data monthly to better track my finances. 
That is what pushed me to make this my side project!

## What I want to learn from this

### Agentic AI
I am particularly interested in how AI can help us code in a systematic way. I have heard
about agents and skills and thus I wanted to give them a try and explore how they actually
work! The skill that I will be utilising is [`superpowers`](https://github.com/obra/superpowers). 
I will be making use of `TDD`, following a **red-green-refactor** cycle, alongside the **plan and execute** 
methodology used in agentic AI workflows.

### Claude Design
Initially, I wanted to try practicing UI design with `Figma` since I have only used `Figma` twice
and most of the design were done by my teammates. However, since Claude Design is here and I am
already juggling with internship and Security+ preparation, I wanted to give Claude Design a try!

So far, the results are incredible, it is able to do even the routing and animations in the
UI design, even though sometimes it will repeat some mistakes in the animation, I found it useful
to provide a **screen recording** of the problem and an example of what I want it to achieve.

This is a unique experience to me but I think I would still want to give `Figma` a try in terms of
actual, original creativity. With that said, Claude Design definitely accelerated the learning path
for building the frontend for applications.

### SQL
I have learned about database and SQL in my last semester and thus I wanted to experience making use
of them in an actual project. Previously, I have always hosted my database on platforms like `Supabase`
that handle it for me but I want to give it a try on my own!
